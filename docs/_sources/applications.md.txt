Applications
============

In this chapter we collect articles and chapters from several 
practical domains (macro, finance, risk and microeconomics) 
for study and replication.

Macro: 
   - GDP
   - FX
   - interest rates
   - asset class allocation 
   - business cycle 

Risks and finance:
   - VAR    
   - search of invariants (Atillo Meucci)

Panel data:
   - *MHE*
   - policy assessment
